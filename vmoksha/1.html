Ayurvedic Doctors in Bangalore

Ayurveda, the history of which travels back a few thousand years, aims to promote good health instead of fighting diseases although treatments may be offered towards particular health issues. People seeking Ayurvedic treatments may consider visiting one of the Ayurvedic doctors as they are skilled professionals in this particular medical branch. Ayurvedic Doctors in Bangalore have their clinics in favourable locations. Ayurvedic herbs are widely used to treat a large number of problems and diseases such as rheumatoid arthritis, anxiety, asthma, skin problems and heart disease. Ayurvedic medical practitioners suggest a balanced and healthy diet, bring necessary lifestyle changes, offer stress relief and recommend plenty of herbal remedies so as to heal conditions and attain balance in the body. The medicines prescribed by Ayurvedic doctors are easily available in the Ayurvedic pharmacy stores of the city. Ayurvedic doctors in Bangalore are the best choice for treating the root cause of ailments

Ayurveda, which is an age-old practice of treating people, is one of the most effective ways of diagnosing and curing various diseases. Earlier, Ayurvedic practitioners were known as vaidyas whose treatment focussed on the complete physical, mental, and spiritual well-being of a person. Today, the various Ayurvedic treatments can be broadly classified into a variety of therapies and stages. The uses of herbs, panchakarma, herbal formulas and changes in the diet are the most common approaches used by Ayurvedic doctors in Bangalore for treating various ailments.

Herbs and formulas

The different kinds of Ayurvedic herbs which are prescribed by Ayurvedic consultants are known for their nutritious qualities. Some of the herbs have to be sourced from abroad. A lot of people are still unaware of the medicinal benefits of these plants. The effectiveness of each of these herbs and plants can be best understood by an Ayurvedic physician. They have a great knowledge of plants and herbs which help them to treat different kinds of health conditions.

Panchakarma

The process of Panchakarma includes a variety of processes such as nasal treatment, steam treatment, vamana, etc. All of these techniques help to clean the body toxins. Such a process should always be performed under the supervision of an experienced Ayurvedic specialist. It is, therefore, important that every person consults trained Ayurvedic doctors and not consume Ayurvedic medicines without proper consultation.

Diet and nutrition

The way people eat and what they have in their meals have a big impact on their lives. A lot of people are known to fall sick because of a lack of physical and mental balance and also an improper lifestyle. Everyone must follow a healthy diet to remain fit throughout their life. For healthy living, it is important to follow an Ayurvedic diet recommended by a certified Ayurvedic doctor. The right kind of diet will not only improve the body system but also make the body responsive to treatments.

Why should people consider seeking an Ayurvedic treatment?

Ayurvedic doctors have extensive knowledge. They not only examine the body but also consider other aspects of their patient’s life to identify the underlying cause of any disorder. They adopt a holistic approach which helps to cure the problem from its roots and also prevents a relapse. Here are some other reasons why people should consult trained Ayurvedic doctors for different health conditions.

Lifestyle and dietary modifications

The science of Ayurveda emphasizes living a balanced life. Ayurvedic doctors help their patients understand the significance of adequate sleep, a healthy diet, and a healthy lifestyle. A poor lifestyle, diet, and sleep can result in health problems.

Tailor-made solutions

Ayurvedic doctors offer personalized treatment to their patients based on their health issues. They prescribe medicines as per the constitution of their patients.

Better diagnosis

Ayurvedic doctors Bangalore has, make use of pulse diagnosis which is considered to be one of the most accurate methods to assess the physiology and imbalances in a person.

Host of home remedies

Ayurvedic physicians have an excellent knowledge of various home remedies for multiple health problems.

Provide easy precautions

Ayurvedic consultants, in addition to treating various health issues, also prevent recurrence of the same. According to them, practising yoga, avoiding certain food items, and living a holistic life can protect the body from a large number of diseases.

Safe medications

The medicines recommended by certified Ayurvedic doctors are safe because they are made from herbal or a combination of herbal and mineral formulations. They generally do not have a side-effect as happens with many allopathic medicines. Ayurveda provides natural solutions to a large number of health issues including chronic pain, metabolic problems, stress, etc.

Significance of yoga and meditation in Ayurveda

In Bangalore Ayurvedic doctors often recommend their patients to perform yoga every day to relax muscles and strengthen their body. The health benefits of yoga are immense. A lot of times, Ayurvedic practitioners suggest their patients for treatments in an Ayurvedic resort because these resorts offer a great opportunity to practice yoga in an extraordinary setting. Meditation helps in mental relaxation. It helps people to control their thoughts and emotions. It also enhances mindfulness. Paying close attention to the breathing techniques and postures is very important during meditation.

Powerful and effective Ayurvedic therapies

There are a plethora of Ayurvedic therapies which may be suggested by Ayurvedic doctors depending on the specific health issue a person is looking for help with. All of these therapies are a part of Panchakarma treatment.

Narangakizhi

Beneficial for: sciatica spondylosis, low backache, acute inflammatory arthritis

Navarakizhi

Beneficial for: nervous degeneration, fatigue/overall weakness, stroke, numbness and burning sensation in the body, muscle wasting

Ksheeradhara

Beneficial for: headaches and insomnia, improve memory and concentration, pitha diseases, mental stress and anxiety

Udwarthanam

Beneficial for: reducing body weight, early stages of nervous and muscular diseases, skin discolouration and scars, inflammatory joint diseases

Thalam

Beneficial for: stroke, insomnia, cervical spondylosis, high blood pressure, stress and mental disorders, facial paralysis

Kateevasthy

Beneficial for: restricted movements of the joints, pain on the neck, lower back and chest, osteoporosis and other degenerations

Dhanyamladhara

Beneficial for: gout and other inflammatory arthritis, rheumatic fever, acute stages of hemiplegia, inflammatory stages of other arthritis

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are the Ayurvedic treatments provided by Ayurvedic doctors in Bangalore?

Some of the well-known Ayurvedic treatments provided by Ayurvedic doctors nearby include Nasyam (nasally administered treatments), Uzhichil or Abhyanga (oil massage), Tharpanam (treatment for the eyes), Pichu, Sirovasthy, Dhanyamladhara, Thalam, etc.

2. What are the benefits of Panchakarma?

Panchkarma, offered by trained Ayurvedic doctors in Bangalore, helps relax the mind and body. It rids the body of toxins, restores the natural balance of the body, regulates metabolism, rejuvenates the skin and tissues, cleanses and enhances the digestive system, and also boosts immunity.

3. What types of disorders can be treated by Ayurvedic doctors near me?

Ayurveda practitioners offer Ayurvedic treatment for a vast range of disorders including anxiety, asthma, arthritis, digestive problems, eczema, high blood pressure, high cholesterol levels, rheumatoid arthritis, stress, etc.

4. What are the types of Ayurvedic therapies?

Ayurvedic doctors may employ a variety of Ayurvedic techniques including changes in the diet, herbal medicine, massage therapy, meditation, breathing exercises, yoga, sound therapy, Panchakarma, and so on. The type of treatment required will vary based on the specific health concerns of the individual.

5. Is Ayurvedic treatment safe?

When you seek Ayurvedic treatment from a certified Ayurvedic doctor, it is considered safe.

6. How is the diagnosis done by Ayurvedic doctors?

The main modes of physical examination include inspection, interrogation, and palpation. The specialist may also assess the strength and physical status of the individual before they can plan the type of management required.
